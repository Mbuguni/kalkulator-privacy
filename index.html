Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22

This Privacy Policy describes how Uchumi Imara Calculator ("the App") handles your information.

The App is designed to work offline and does not require account registration. By default, the App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data and we do not share personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics.

4. Permissions

The App may request only the permissions needed for normal operation and sharing exported reports.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the App secure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
